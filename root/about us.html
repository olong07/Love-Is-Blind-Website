Here at love is blind, we aim to give pre-loved books to a new home, this allows there to be less paper waste and allows people to save money. There are not enough young people reading anymore so we aim to help increase reading in the younger generations. This will greatly improve their literacy skills and vocabulary. Using bio-degradable paper which can easily be found on amazon, we are attempting to lessen the strain on the environment even further. We use reputable brands such as Triplast in order to minimise the impact we leave on the environment.
